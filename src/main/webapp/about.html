About

This is my very first project using Lift and Scala. It was inspired by my large student loans.

To use this loan calculator, input your loans into the fields and select a strategy you want to use to pay them off.

When you press Calculate, the underlying optimization algorithms will calculate the best monthly payments you can make to your loans.

The strategies you can choose are:
  • Snowball
  • Avalanche
  • Lazy
Sorry, I didn't make up these names.
